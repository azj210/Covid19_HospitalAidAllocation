• US Hospital Capacity by County
After calculating new cases/deaths every day for each county starting from 3/22, I took the calculated data and fit the data set using a least squares polynomial fit function with days as the independent variable and cases or deaths as the dependent variable. Taking the coefficients that minimize squared error, I used the resultant equation to predict new cases/deaths based on days since 3/22.

  • Aggregate Hospital & Demographics Data
Hospital Quality is defined by reported deaths from: COPD, Pneumonia, and Postoperative Respiratory Failure. These measures were used since Covid19 is a respiratory disease. The number provided is the deviation of a county's hospitals' deathrate from the national average with positive deviation being better and negative deviation being worse.
For population data, individuals over the age of 55 were included since that group is the most susceptible to deaths and hospitalization from Covid19.
